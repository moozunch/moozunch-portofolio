Donday

UI/UX Designer, Front End and User Experience Controller

Donday is an innovative platform designed to streamline the blood donation process by connecting donors with individuals in need. With advanced features such as a proximity-based search and an integrated forum, locating a donor is now more efficient and convenient, significantly reducing wait times. Additionally, our in-app chat ensures secure, private, and reliable communication between donors and recipients, placing a strong emphasis on privacy and trust.

This project successfully won 3rd place in DSC Solve> organized by the Google Developer Student Club Medan.
